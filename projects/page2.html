Chicago Bulls 1976-1984

Artis Gilmore, acquired in the ABA dispersal draft in 1976, led the Bulls. In 1979, the Bulls lost a coin flip for the right to select first in the NBA draft, as a result they weren't able to draft future hall of famer Magic Johnson. During this time the Bulls didn't have the brightest days, but something changed and they became the best team of all time.

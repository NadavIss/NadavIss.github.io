Missing the playoffs

During the off season, the Bulls signed forward Joe Smith and guard Adrian Griffin, and drafted center Joakim Noah. However, distractions began when Luol Deng and Ben Gordon turned down contract extensions, never citing reasons. Then rumors surfaced that the Bulls were pursuing stars like Kevin Garnett, Pau Gasol, and most notably, Kobe Bryant. None of these deals happened. The Bulls started the 2007–08 NBA season by losing 10 of their first 12 games. The Bulls ended the 2007–08 campaign with a 33–49 record, a complete reversal of last year's record.

Chicago Bulls 1966-1976

The Chicago Bulls team was founded on January 16th 1966. In between 1966 and 1976 not much happened because they were just starting, but as they played more seasons they got more fans. The Bulls had four 50-win seasons in between 1966 and 1976, and they were led by Jerry Sloan and Bob Love.
